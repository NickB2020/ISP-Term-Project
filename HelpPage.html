How To Play

Click START to start the game.
You are the blue circle, point and click above the incoming ememies to make them disappear and earn points.
The score is kept on the top. After the game ends, add the score to the database by scrolling down and adding a score ID and the number of points achieved and clicking the "add your score button" then click "view scores". You can also delete a score by entering the same info and clicking the "delete" button then click "view scores".
Do not let the enemies touch the blue cirlce or game over.
